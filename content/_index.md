I'm a backend engineer with over a decade of experience in large-scale distributed systems. In recent years, my focus has shifted toward AI applications — exploring how intelligence can be embedded into real products and workflows.

Outside of work, I run, read, and trade stocks. I'm drawn to psychology — understanding why we make the decisions we do, especially under pressure. These interests may seem scattered, but they share a thread: understanding complex systems, whether they're built with code, shaped by markets, or wired into the human mind.

This website is my Digital Garden — a place where I document projects, write technical notes, share reading reflections, and track what I'm learning over time. Not everything here is polished. Some ideas are seeds, some are still growing. That's the point.
